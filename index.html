Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh
Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of bangladesh who is especially recogized as Bidrohi Kobi(Rebel Poet) around the country. Nazrul is the pfamous poet, musician and philosopher of Bangldesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writings deal with the revolution of that period of the british government. His revelutionary attempt helped him to be the national poet of the Bangladesh. He has raised his voice againest any kinds of fascism and appression and got popularity swiptly around the Indian subcontinent

Awsome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and revelotion. He apposed all forms of bigotry, and fundamentalism, including religious, caste-based and gender-based, throught his career, Nazrul wrote the short stories, novels and essays but is best known for his songs and pems. he pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(Many recored on HMV and gramophone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
